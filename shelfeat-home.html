Join the Fight Against Food Waste

At ShelfEat, we're committed to reducing food waste while providing accessible solutions for consumers and businesses alike. By aligning with the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goal (SDG) 12: Responsible Consumption and Production, we aim to promote sustainable business practices that benefit both the environment and society.

Our platform helps businesses optimize their inventory and reduce excess stock, while consumers gain access to affordable, quality products that would otherwise go to waste.

Through our efforts, we contribute to the global movement to cut food losses during manufacturing and supply chains, as well as to cut food waste in half per person at the retail and consumer levels, in such way we promote a circular economy and help create a more sustainable future for everybody by enabling companies and individuals to take action.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: Our mission is to develop an innovative, user-friendly platform that connects food industries with buyers by collecting ready-to-expire goods.

Vision: Our vision is to build an inclusive society where every individual has easier access to affordable, essential products.
