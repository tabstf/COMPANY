About Shelfeat

Meet Shelf Eat, a Student Company with a Mission;

We are Shelf Eat, a team of 19 IB students from Anatolia College, participating in the "Εικονική Επιχείρηση" program by Junior Achievement Greece! Our mission? To reduce food waste and make sustainable shopping accessible to everyone.

By partnering with grocery stores, we rescue high-quality products near their expiration date and offer them at discounted prices, helping consumers save money while reducing food waste. Our goal is to promote conscious consumption, support economical solutions, and contribute to a greener planet.

Catch us at the trade fair on March 7th at Cosmos as we showcase our innovative idea and commitment to sustainability!

Our Team

Our Mission

Our aim is to make shopping for sustainable products easy and affordable for everyone. We work with various supermarkets to preserve high-quality products that are approaching their expiration date and offer them at lower prices. In this way, we aim to combat food waste, promote conscious consumption and offer economical solutions to consumers in need of cheaper products, while contributing to a greener planet.

Our Vision

We envision a future where food waste is minimized and sustainable shopping becomes normal. Shelf Eat strives to be a pioneer in ethical food sales, creating a world where businesses collaborate with consumers through their responsible and ecological consumer choices, thus helping the environment.
